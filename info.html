About

Not Mister Purple is a band that was formed on a dark and stormy night by a group of musically skilled students jamming together early in their freshman year. Fueled by their shared passion for songwriting and performing, they broke into the campus music scene, making their debut at the Kelly Writers House. With Not Mister Purple’s repertoire containing an eclectic amalgamation of genres, ranging anywhere from classic rock to hyperpop, it’s hard to pin down exactly who they are. But, it’s clear who they are not.
